About Me

Born in Nigeria and having lived in the Kaduna State for over two decades before settling in a serene village in Enugu State near the border, I've blended Eastern roots with Northern influences to build robust, user-centric digital solutions. My life outside code, Nordic walking through lush forests with my dog Bubbles, hitting the gym for group sessions, and unwinding in saunas. It keeps me grounded, fueling my creativity and problem-solving stamina.

As a full-stack wizard, I thrive at the intersection of front-end flair and back-end muscle. On the front end, I'm all about crafting seamless, responsive UIs with React, Vue.js, and Next.js, ensuring pixel-perfect designs via Tailwind CSS or Bootstrap that adapt flawlessly across devices. I love turning complex ideas into intuitive experiences, incorporating animations with Framer Motion or accessibility best practices to make apps inclusive and engaging.

I automate deployments with Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD pipelines via GitHub Actions or Jenkins,ensuring smooth, zero-downtime updates. Over the years, I've tackled diverse projects: from e-commerce platforms that boosted sales by 40% through personalized recommendations, to AI-driven tools leveraging TensorFlow for predictive analytics, and even mobile hybrids with React Native. My entrepreneurial spirit led me to side hustles in freelancing on Upwork, where I've delivered custom solutions for startups in fintech and e-learning, always prioritizing clean, maintainable code and agile methodologies like Scrum.
